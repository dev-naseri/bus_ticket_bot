# Project Goals and Non-Goals

## Project Goals

- A ticket sales service designed for small to medium-sized groups or companies.
- Enable end-to-end ticket reservation through a Telegram bot.
- Ease of use and reduced project complexity for users without technical expertise.

## Non-Goals

- This project is not intended for large-scale usage.
- It is not designed to support a high number of concurrent users.

## Success Criteria

- A smooth and successful purchase experience for users.
- Efficient and accurate management of tickets and sales for transit companies or groups

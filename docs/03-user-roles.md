# User Roles
- This system defines two primary roles: Passenger and Admin.

## Passenger (Buyer)

### Responsibilities
- Ticket selection
- Submission of personal information
- Submission of payment receipt

### Capabilities
- Viewing active tickets
- Reservation cancellation
- Viewing payment status

### Limitations
- No modification of information after confirmation
- Time-limited cancellation policy
- No access to other usersâ€™ information

---

## Admin

### Responsibilities
- Definition of time slots and capacity
- Payment approval and verification
- Cancellation management
- Generating detailed reports of ticket purchase transactions for passenger management
- Maintaining a complete record of all completed transactions within a designated Telegram transaction group

### Capabilities
- Viewing all reservations
- Data export functionality
- Updating ticket status

### Limitations
- No modification of finalized transactions
- No deletion of reservation history
- Restricted access to sensitive settings (if applicable)
